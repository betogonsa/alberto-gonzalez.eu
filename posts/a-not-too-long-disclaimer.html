A (not-too-long) disclaimer

June 03, 2025 · Originally published on The mobility climate

Welcome!

Here you will find my personal reflections on transport, mobility. sustainability, and climate. Most of the content here will focus on the European Union – particularly Spain. All post will be written in English, aimed at the small niche of people interested in mobility in Spain, in English.

I have the privilege of working at the European Investment Bank as a Senior Transport Specialist. My work focuses, internally, on horizontal matters and, externally, of technical advisory. Every day, I engage with great people with even better ideas.

This Substack is my attempt to capture some of those ideas.

Please note: the views presented here are entirely my own. They do not represent the positions of my employer, nor those of the stakeholders and clients in work with. Each post will start with a disclaimer – just to be on the safe side.

From time to time, I might argue a position strongly, even passionately, perhaps even vehemently... just to later realize that the issue was more complex or nuanced. So, it is possible that, by the time you read a post, may views may have evolved and the post might no longer represent even myself. I hope that some of that evolution might result from engaging with you.

I intend to use AI to proofread the texts – but I can promise I will not dump here purely AI-generated text. In fact, the first drafts of each post will be handwritten – with a fountain pen and green ink.

I will try to publish weekly, but only if I have something that is worthy.

Finally, even if it makes me flush a little, I confess I would really love if you would subscribe.

Thanks!
